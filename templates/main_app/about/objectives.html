OBJECTIVES

The following are the objectives of the associations:-

  • To safeguard operating rights of FOSAS
  • To provide lobby, advice in liaison with KUSCO LTD
  • To Protect from unfavorable conditional ties emanating from local and international mainstream financial institutions and others
  • To Promote ethical and sound practices in the management of FOSAS
  • To share information concerning FOSAS and promote education and training of members, officials and identified stakeholders
  • To ensure compliance with Act, Rules and Cooperative principles
  • To assist in achieving the above objectives, of the association to adhere.
  • To work closely with NACOS
  • Shall be non-political
