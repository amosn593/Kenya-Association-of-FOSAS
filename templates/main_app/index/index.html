Kenya Association of Front Office Activity(KAFOSA) formerly known as Coast Association of front office services activities (CAFOSA) was registered in February 2010 to provide lobby and advocacy to deposit taking Sacco’s in Kenya.

Exactly two years after realizing this dream it became necessary to widen our scope to reach out to more Sacco’s hence the registration of the Kenyan chapter in February 2012, mainly to create an enabling environment for Sacco’s with Fosas in their areas of operation and to play a similar role as is the case with KBA – Kenya Bankers Association and AMFI –Association of Micro Finance Institutions.

Due to emerging challenges brought about as a result of the regulators compliance requirements, which some of the system vendors took advantage of to exploit our members and potential members, meeting the minimum Capital requirement amongst many others challenges.

Our Mission

To foster strong links amongst FOSA operators by offering protection to the membership, sharing information with view to enhance product development for our customers.

Vision

To be the most reliable most informative association for FOSAs in Kenya.

Core Values
  • Intergrity
  • Efficiency
  • Awareness
  • Progress
