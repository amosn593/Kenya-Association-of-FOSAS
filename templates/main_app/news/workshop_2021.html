KAFOSA WORKSHOP ON 15TH - 18TH SEPTEMBER 2021

The KAFOSA workshop held at Turtle Bay Hotel on 15th –18th September 2021 . The cooperative/Sacco sub sectors assets stood at 622 billion in 2019 up from 556 million posting a growth of 12% per annum and hence the need to put in more measures that will safeguard members savings as the regulator puts new regulations for non-deposit taking Saccos to be complied with before being authorized.

The theme of the 2021 workshop was “ICT IN ADVANCING FINANCIAL INCLUSION FOR SACCOS”:

Some of the topics discussed included:

  • Enhancing Sacco sector growth
  • Business Remodeling
  • Aligning the value chain of a Sacco to the contemporary ICT ecosystem
  • Governance, up-skilling IT managers
  • Developing skills for a financial industry
  • Cyber Security, Data Protection Laws
  • Re-evaluation of your vendor relationship in safeguarding your cooperative
  • Meet vendors with new & better solutions
