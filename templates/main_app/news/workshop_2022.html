KAFOSA WORKSHOP ON 5TH - 7TH MAY 2022

Entrepreneurial vision is very critical in making a business achieve better growth and development. When covid-19 was at its peak in the country many SACCOs grappled with how to continue giving services and the ones related with aviation, hospitality and other industries tapped into their savings to sustain themselves as they were negatively affected.

Through use of Technology SACCOs have managed to continue serving their members as they innovate around new ways to survive and thrive. The digital consumption was at its all-time highest during the pandemic and technology aided operations in many sectors.

ICT as an excellent enabler plays a pivotal role in enhancing efficiency resulting in profitability of SACCOs through components such as cost reduction, cheaper distribution channels and reduction in supply time, innovation and good customer services.

Some of the challenges the subsector experiences are; Uptake of the system and awareness challenges, Technical challenges, and Human resource challenges, Stakeholder challenges, Cyber security threats.

The theme of the 2022 workshop was “ICT AS AN ENABLER OF ENTREPRENEURAL VISION AND GROWTH”

Participants were drawn from across the country involving all cooperatives/SACCOs including those planning to start DT services, those operating DT, and Counties ICT professionals and other relevant departments.
